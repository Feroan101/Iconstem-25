About The Conference


The Scope of the Conference is to provide a premier platform for researchers, practitioners, and professionals worldwide to exchange knowledge and foster innovations at the intersection of Intelligence and Technology in Next Gen. With rapid advancements in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Internet of Things (IoT), Data Analytics, and emerging technologies, the Conference will explore how these fields are revolutionizing Industries, Society and Health Care.

It will also encourage discussions on interdisciplinary research, collaboration between academia and industry, in shaping the future of intelligent technologies in Next Gen. Workshops, keynote sessions, and paper presentations will provide in-depth insights into cutting-edge developments, with a focus on real-world applications and solutions that address global challenges in the Next Gen.
